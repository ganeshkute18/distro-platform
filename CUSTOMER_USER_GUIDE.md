## Getting Started

### Sign Up (First Time)

**If you're new to DistroPro**:

1. **Open DistroPro** in your browser or download the app
2. **Click "Sign Up"** on login page
3. **Fill in your details**:
   - **Full Name**: Your name
   - **Email Address**: For order confirmations and updates
   - **Phone Number**: So the supplier can contact you
   - **Password**: Create a secure password
   - **Delivery Address**: Where orders should go
     - Building name/number
     - Street name
     - City, State, PIN code
4. **Click "Create Account"**
5. **Confirmation email** sent to your email
6. **Done!** You can now browse and order

### Login (Returning Customer)

1. **Go to login page**
2. **Enter email and password**
3. **Click "Sign In"**
4. **You're in!** Your dashboard shows your orders and notifications

### First Time Buyer Tip

✅ **Complete your profile**:
- Add a clear delivery address (use your shop/business address)
- Add phone number (so supplier can contact if needed)
- This helps orders arrive correctly the first time

## Managing Your Cart

### What's a Cart?

Your **Cart** is temporary:
- Like a shopping basket
- Add/remove items before ordering
- Doesn't reserve products
- Products might sell out while in your cart

### Adding to Cart

**To add a product**:

1. **View product details**
2. **Enter quantity**:
   - Example: Want 5 boxes? Enter "5"
   - System shows unit type: "5 BOX"
3. **Click "Add to Cart"**
4. ✅ **Product added** (see confirmation)

### Viewing Your Cart

**To review items before ordering**:

1. **Click Cart icon** (usually top right, shows 🛒)
2. **See all items**:
   - Product name
   - Quantity ordered
   - Price per unit
   - Subtotal per product
3. **See total amount** at bottom
4. **Options**:
   - Remove item (trash icon)
   - Change quantity (+ and - buttons)
   - Continue shopping (back to catalog)
   - Proceed to checkout (place order)

### Editing Cart

**Change quantity**:

1. **In cart, find product**
2. **Click + to add more**
3. **Click - to reduce**
4. **Quantity updates** instantly
5. **Total price recalculates** automatically

**Remove item**:

1. **In cart, find product**
2. **Click trash/remove icon**
3. **Item removed** from cart
4. Can add it back anytime

### Cart Tips

✅ **Pro Tips**:
- Add multiple items from different suppliers (mixed orders ok)
- Set delivery date based on when you need items
- Review prices - compare between suppliers if available
- Check if item is featured or promoted before checkout

## Placing an Order

### Step-by-Step Order Process

#### Step 1: Review Your Cart

```
Your Cart (Example)
─────────────────────
Milk (1L) × 10 = ₹450
Bread × 2 = ₹120
Butter × 1 = ₹280
─────────────────────
Subtotal: ₹850
```

Check everything looks correct ✅

#### Step 2: Choose Delivery Date

1. **Tap "Select Delivery Date"** field
2. **Pick-a-date calendar opens**
3. **Choose when you want delivery**:
   - Today (if same-day available)
   - Tomorrow
   - Specific date next week
4. **Confirm date**

**Delivery dates vary**:
- Different suppliers have different schedules
- Urban areas: often same-day or next-day
- Rural areas: might be 2-3 days
- Peak days: longer delays

#### Step 3: Review Delivery Address

1. **Check address shown**:
   - Is it correct?
   - Building name?
   - Full address complete?
2. **Change if needed**:
   - Click "Edit Address"
   - Update delivery location
   - Save
3. **Add delivery notes** (optional):
   - "Ring doorbell on 3rd floor"
   - "Gate outside, no entry to building"
   - "Call before arriving"

#### Step 4: Choose Payment Method

**Available options** (depending on supplier):

| Method | How It Works | When You Pay |
|--------|-------------|--------------|
| **Cash on Delivery** | Pay driver when order arrives | When received |
| **Bank Transfer** | Send money to supplier's account | Before/after delivery |
| **Digital Wallet** | UPI, Google Pay, PayTM, etc. | Right now |

**Choose what works for you**

#### Step 5: Review & Confirm

1. **See order summary**:
   - Items and quantities
   - Delivery date
   - Address
   - Payment method
   - **Total amount**
2. **Everything correct?** ✅
3. **Click "Place Order"**
4. **Order submitted** to supplier! 🎉

### What Happens Next

```
Your Order Placed ✅
    ↓
📊 Status: "PENDING_APPROVAL" (Supplier reviewing)
    ↓
⏳ Wait for supplier to approve
    ↓
When approved:
✅ "APPROVED" - Order confirmed
    ↓
📦 Status: "PROCESSING" (Being packed)
    ↓
📮 Status: "DISPATCHED" (Out for delivery)
    ↓
✅ Status: "DELIVERED" (You received it!)
```

You'll get **notifications** at each step.

### Order Status Explained

| Status | Meaning | What's Happening |
|--------|---------|------------------|
| **PENDING_APPROVAL** | 🕐 Waiting for supplier to review | Supplier reviewing your order |
| **APPROVED** | ✅ Confirmed! | Supplier accepted, will prepare |
| **PROCESSING** | 📦 Being prepared | Staff packing your items |
| **DISPATCHED** | 📮 On the way | Order sent out for delivery |

## Payment Methods

### Cash on Delivery (COD)

**How it works**:
1. Place order
2. Supplier delivers products
3. You verify items are correct
4. You pay the driver in cash
5. Driver gives receipt

**Pros**: ✅ No risk, see before paying
**Cons**: ❌ Need exact cash or change ready

### Bank Transfer

**How it works**:
1. Place order
2. Supplier provides bank account details
3. You transfer money (online banking)
4. Send screenshot/proof to supplier
5. Supplier confirms receipt
6. Delivery happens

**Pros**: ✅ Official record, safe
**Cons**: ❌ Need bank account, transfer takes time

### Digital Payment (UPI / Wallets)

**Available on most phones**:

- **UPI**: Google Pay, PhonePe, BHIM, Paytm
- **Wallets**: Google Pay, Paytm, Amazon Pay
- **Apps**: WhatsApp Pay (if available)

**How it works**:
1. Place order
2. Choose "Digital Payment"
3. Scan QR code or enter UPI ID
4. Payment goes through instantly
5. Order confirmed immediately
6. Delivery scheduled

**Pros**: ✅ Instant, no waiting
**Cons**: ❌ Need money in account, internet required

### Which Method to Choose?

- **First time with supplier?** → Cash on Delivery (safer)
- **Trusted supplier?** → Digital payment (faster)
- **Large order?** → Bank transfer (official record)
- **Regular buyer?** → Digital payment (quickest)

## Notifications

### What Notifications Tell You

**You get alerts for**:

| Alert | When | Action |
|-------|------|--------|

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Is my payment information safe?**
- A: Yes! We use bank-level security (HTTPS encryption)
- A: We never store credit card details
- A: Only approved payment gateways used

**Q: Can I cancel an order?**
- A: ✅ If PENDING_APPROVAL: Yes, contact supplier
- A: ❌ If APPROVED/PROCESSING: Difficult, contact supplier
- A: ❌ If already DISPATCHED: No, but you can refuse delivery

**Q: How long does delivery take?**
- A: Depends on location and supplier
- A: Urban areas: Same day or next day
- A: Can take 2-3 days for remote areas
- A: Check during checkout (shows estimated date)

**Q: What if I don't receive my order?**
- A: Contact the supplier immediately
- A: Check your address is correct in order
- A: Ask driver to re-attempt delivery
- A: If real issue: supplier gives refund/replacement

**Q: Can I change delivery address after ordering?**
- A: ✅ If PENDING_APPROVAL: Maybe (ask supplier)
- A: ❌ If already PROCESSING: Too late, contact supplier
- A: Better to cancel and reorder with new address

**Q: Do you store my address data?**
- A: Yes, to process deliveries
- A: Can edit/delete in Settings anytime
- A: Not shared with anyone except your supplier

### Product Questions

**Q: What if product is out of stock?**
- A: Show as ❌ "Out of Stock" in catalog
- A: Can't add to cart
- A: Check back later if you want
- A: Some suppliers notify when back in stock

**Q: What if product I ordered is no longer available?**
- A: Supplier will reject the order
- A: You'll get notification
- A: Can try different brand or product
- A: Refund given if paid in advance

**Q: Can I order multiple products from different suppliers?**
- A: ✅ Yes! No restrictions
- A: Each supplier prepares separately
- A: Deliveries might be on different dates

**Q: What if I receive wrong product/quantity?**
- A: Contact supplier immediately with photos
- A: They'll usually send replacement
- A: Some give refund if product no longer needed
- A: Keep packaging/receipt for proof

### Payment Questions

**Q: Can I use a prepaid debit card?**
- A: ✅ Yes! If digital payment available
- A: ❌ Not for COD (needs cash)

**Q: What if payment failed?**
- A: Order goes to PENDING
- A: Try payment again
- A: Contact supplier for payment link resend
- A: Switch to different payment method

**Q: Is there a minimum order amount?**
- A: Depends on supplier
- A: Some have ₹500 minimum
- A: Some have no minimum
- A: Check during checkout

**Q: Can I get invoice/receipt?**
- A: ✅ Yes! In order details
- A: Click "Download Invoice"
- A: Good for business records/GST

### Delivery Questions

**Q: What if no one is home for delivery?**
- A: Driver will try to contact you
- A: May leave at security/neighbor (you note it)
- A: Can ask driver to come back later
- A: Delivery rescheduled if not possible

**Q: Can I schedule delivery for specific time?**
- A: Depends on supplier
- A: Some offer time slots (9-5 PM, etc.)
- A: Others give date range only
- A: Check during checkout what's available

**Q: What areas do you deliver to?**
- A: Each supplier has different zones
- A: Enter your address at checkout
- A: System shows if deliverable or not
- A: Check delivery charge if outside main area

## Shopping Tips

💡 **Smart Shopping**:
